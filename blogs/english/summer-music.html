Music Trends Shaping Summer 2024

As we dive into the music trends defining summer 2024, it’s clear that this season is packed with vibrant sounds and innovative styles. From the infectious beats of dance pop to the soulful melodies of country music, this summer’s music landscape offers something for everyone. Let’s explore the trending music this summer and the key elements driving these trends.

Dance Pop: The Unstoppable Force

One of the standout genres this summer is dance pop. Leading the charge is Charli XCX with her hit album “Brat,” released on June 7, 2024. The album has quickly become a cultural phenomenon, especially with the breakout hit “Apple”. This track, along with several remixes, has dominated the charts and social media platforms.

  • Key Songs and Collaborations:
    • "Apple": A viral sensation, particularly on TikTok, with countless dance challenges.
    • "Girl, So Confusing" Remix: Featuring Lorde, released on June 21, 2024.
    • "Guess" Remix: Featuring Billie Eilish, released on August 1, 2024.

The “brat” aesthetic, inspired by Charli XCX’s album, has resonated deeply with Gen Z and millennials. This aesthetic is characterized by a messy, carefree vibe that embraces imperfections and chaos, making it a significant cultural trend. It’s not just the music but the entire ethos surrounding it, including fashion trends like the popular “brat green”, which has overtaken last summer’s “Barbie pink”.

The “brat” trend has made a significant impact on summer 2024’s cultural landscape, influencing everything from music to fashion.

Country Music: A Global Phenomenon

While dance pop is making waves, country music has also seen a remarkable surge in popularity. This genre, traditionally rooted in American culture, has expanded its reach globally.

  • Key Artist and Song:
    • Shaboozey: His track “A Bar Song (Tipsy)” has become an international hit, topping Spotify charts in countries such as the US, Australia, Canada, Ireland, Norway, and Sweden. The song first hit No. 1 on July 4, 2024.

The success of Shaboozey’s track underscores a broader trend of country music gaining traction outside its traditional markets. According to Emma Vikström, Spotify’s Editorial Lead for the Nordics, the growth of country music on Spotify’s Hot Hits playlists worldwide is unprecedented.

Shaboozey’s “A Bar Song (Tipsy)” exemplifies the global rise of country music, breaking into new markets and resonating with diverse audiences.

Conclusion

In summary, the music trends shaping summer 2024 are a testament to the evolving tastes of global audiences. With dance pop and country music at the forefront, this summer’s trending music offers a dynamic mix of sounds and styles. Whether you’re dancing to the beats of Charli XCX or enjoying the nostalgic vibes of Shaboozey, there’s no shortage of summer anthems to define your season.

Creating Your Own Summer Playlist

Tips on How to Create a Personalized Summer Playlist

Creating a summer music playlist can be a fun and rewarding experience. Here are some tips to help you craft the perfect collection of songs:

  1. Consider the Mood:

    • Think about the overall vibe you want your playlist to convey. Whether it’s upbeat and energetic for beach parties or mellow and relaxing for evening chill-outs, the mood sets the tone for your song selection.

  2. Activities and Settings:

    • Tailor your playlist to specific activities such as road trips, pool parties, or barbecues. Each activity might call for a different type of music.

  3. Personal Taste:

    • Include your favorite genres and artists. Mixing well-known hits with hidden gems can make your playlist unique and enjoyable.

  4. Mix of Popular Hits and Hidden Gems:

    • Combine chart-toppers like “Espresso” by Sabrina Carpenter and “Not Like Us” by Kendrick Lamar with lesser-known tracks to keep the playlist fresh and interesting.

  5. Cultural Resonance:

    • Choose songs that have a cultural impact or are trending, as these can add a contemporary feel to your playlist.

  6. Diverse Genres:

    • Incorporate a variety of genres to cater to different tastes and keep the playlist dynamic.
